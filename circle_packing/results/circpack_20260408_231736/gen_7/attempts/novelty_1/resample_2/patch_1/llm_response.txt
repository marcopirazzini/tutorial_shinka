2) Tightening the local optimizer's convergence criteria and increasing its capacity (more iterations) so that the joint optimization can better settle into high-quality configurations discovered during basin-hopping.

These edits are incremental and self-contained, preserving the overall structure (three initialization strategies plus a final refinement loop) and keeping the interface intact. They leverage existing helper routines (compute_adaptive_radii, boost_radii, optimize_packing) without introducing external dependencies.
